About this project!

USA vs CANADA go head to head in the most anticipated boxing championship since 1997! Both countries have put forward their strongest national animals to fight for a chance to take home the crown and be named BEAST BOXING CHAMPION. Which side are you on?

BEAST BOXING is a game design pack I created for a collaborative art book my friends and I put together. We named it Pick n Mix which we felt best described our differing art styles and how this book has a little bit of something for everyone. We had six weeks to let our creativity run wild with no limits to make whatever we wanted. I had a lot of fun and seeing all the amazing art that has come from this project has been absolutely mind blowing.
